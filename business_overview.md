
Business Overview

Business Name: Inspire Science Institute
Location: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India
Business Type: Science Coaching Institute

Target Audience

Students from Classes 8 to 12 who want strong fundamentals in science subjects and those preparing for board exams or competitive exams such as NEET and JEE foundation.

Business Description

Inspire Science Institute in Coimbatore provides specialized coaching for science subjects including Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. The institute focuses on concept-based learning to help students understand subjects deeply and perform well in exams.

Key Services

Science tuition for Classes 8–12
Physics coaching for board exams
Chemistry coaching for board exams
Biology coaching for NEET foundation
Regular mock tests and revision classes

Unique Selling Points

Experienced science faculty
Concept-focused teaching approach
Small batch sizes for personal attention
Regular tests and performance analysis
Strong focus on exam preparation
