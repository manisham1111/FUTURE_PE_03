 FUTURE_PE_03

 AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

Project Overview

This project demonstrates how Prompt Engineering and AI tools can be used to generate SEO-optimized blog content for business websites.

Many business websites fail to rank on search engines because their blog content is unstructured and not optimized for search intent. This project creates a structured SEO content cluster consisting of one pillar blog and multiple supporting blogs.

Business Example

Business Name: Inspire Science Institute
Location: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India
Business Type: Science Coaching Institute

Objective

The goal of this project is to design prompts that can generate:

SEO blog outlines with clear heading structures
A pillar blog targeting a primary keyword
Supporting blogs connected through internal linking
Local SEO optimized content for business websites

Tools Used

ChatGPT
Google Search (keyword inspiration)
Google Trends
AnswerThePublic

SEO Strategy

Primary Keyword
Best Science Coaching Institute in Coimbatore

Content Cluster

Pillar Blog
Best Science Coaching Institute in Coimbatore – Courses, Benefits & What to Expect

Supporting Blogs
How to Choose the Best Science Tuition in Coimbatore
NEET Foundation Coaching for Class 11 and 12
Tips to Score High in Physics and Chemistry Exams
Why Concept-Based Learning is Important for Science Students

Conclusion

This project shows how AI can help generate structured SEO content that helps businesses improve search visibility and attract potential students.
